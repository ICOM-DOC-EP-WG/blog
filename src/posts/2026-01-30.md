The Exhibition and Documentation Working Group met on January 29th to welcome new participants. The meeting brought together 22 members from Argentina, Canada, Egypt, France, Greece, Indonesia, Italy, the Netherlands, Portugal, Scotland, Singapore, Spain, Sweden, and the United Kingdom.

During this first gathering, the group defined its main objectives for the year, with a focus on formally launching the working group and initiating the development of a shared bibliography as well as a list of ongoing projects related to exhibition and documentation practices.

If you are interested in joining the working group, please fill out the following form: [https://framaforms.org/joining-epd-working-group-icom-1768235763](https://framaforms.org/joining-epd-working-group-icom-1768235763)
